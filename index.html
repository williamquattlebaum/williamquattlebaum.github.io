About

I'm a cybersecurity professional pivoting into my first SOC Analyst role after building hands-on experience the hard way — standing up a real home lab from scratch and breaking (then fixing) things in a controlled environment.

My lab runs on a Dell OptiPlex 7010 (32GB RAM) with Proxmox managing 6 VMs: Wazuh SIEM, Splunk, TheHive, Windows 10 endpoint, Kali Linux attacker, and an AI agent container. I've run red team simulations, built custom detection tooling in Python, documented 6 incident reports, and mapped all findings to MITRE ATT&CK.

I hold a B.S. in Cybersecurity from Kennesaw State University and am completing my M.S. in Cybersecurity & Information Assurance at WGU. I'm Security+ certified and actively studying for Network+.
